# 🚀 Lately - Feature Ideas & Roadmap

This file tracks all feature ideas for Lately. Prioritize and check off as we build!

---

## 🎨 During Preview/Creation

### 1. Smart Photo Swapping
- [ ] AI suggests "this photo is similar but better scored"
- [ ] Swipe up on any photo → see alternatives from same moment
- [ ] One-tap swap

### 2. Auto Color Grading
- [ ] Detect the "vibe" of your dump (warm/cool/moody)
- [ ] Apply consistent filter to all photos
- [ ] Suggest: "These 3 photos have different tones, want to unify?"

### 3. Story Flow Detection
- [ ] AI orders photos by narrative (morning → night)
- [ ] Or by location (home → cafe → park)
- [ ] "Chronological" vs "Curated" toggle

### 4. Cover Photo Picker
- [ ] AI suggests best cover (highest score + face + landscape)
- [ ] Preview how it'll look as Instagram first slide

### 5. Duplicate Killer
- [ ] Detect burst/similar photos
- [ ] "You have 5 similar shots, keep the best one?"

---

## 📱 App-Wide Features

### 6. Photo Memories Timeline
- [ ] "1 year ago you had great photos" notification
- [ ] Resurface old gems automatically

### 7. Smart Widgets
- [ ] "Your week in photos" widget
- [ ] Tap → instant dump creation

### 8. Quick Share to Stories
- [ ] Not just album - direct to Instagram stories
- [ ] Auto-resize for different platforms (TikTok, BeReal)

### 9. Collaborative Dumps
- [ ] Scan friend's QR → both contribute photos
- [ ] Great for trips, parties, events

### 10. AI Captions That Slap
- [ ] Not generic - analyze photos and generate witty captions
- [ ] "Taco Tuesday hits different at 2am" type vibes
